Our 3-Step Fulfillment Process

We do not just ship products; we ensure perfection before your item ever leaves our facility. Here is exactly what happens when you secure a piece with us:

1
Order Preparation
Once your payment (or 50% deposit) is secured via PayPal, Zelle, ApplePay, or Credit Card, our team retrieves your item from our Dubai or Hong Kong vault and handles any requested customizations.
2
Video Approval
Before shipping, we record a high-definition quality-check video of your exact item. We send this to you via DM/WhatsApp. Your order does not ship until you give final approval.
3
FedEx Dispatch
Upon your approval, your package is securely boxed with full premium packaging and dispatched globally via FedEx Expedited Shipping.

Once you have approved your pre-shipment quality video, your package is handed over to FedEx. You can expect your delivery to arrive within 7 Working Days, whether you are in the USA, Canada, Australia, or beyond.
