Privacy Policy

Your trust is our ultimate luxury. Learn how we securely manage, protect, and respect your personal information.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Omega Hub Online, safeguarding your privacy is just as important to us as delivering premium AAA quality pieces. Because we value a highly personalized, VIP client experience, we believe in total transparency regarding how your data is collected, used, and protected when you interact with our website or communicate with us directly.

Information We Collect

Because we operate on a bespoke "DM to Buy" model, the majority of the information we collect is provided entirely voluntarily by you during our one-on-one consultations. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Your name, Instagram handle, WhatsApp number, and email address used for communication.
  • Shipping Details: Your physical delivery address (whether in the USA, Canada, Australia, or worldwide) necessary for FedEx dispatch.
  • Order History: Details regarding the premium items you have inquired about, negotiated, or purchased, allowing us to serve you better in the future.

How We Use Your Information

We do not collect data simply to have it; we collect it exclusively to facilitate our premium white-glove service. Your information is used to:

  • Customize your specific order requests prior to shipment.
  • Send you your personalized Pre-Shipment Quality Approval Video via DM.
  • Generate accurate FedEx shipping labels and provide you with live tracking updates.
  • Process returns, refunds, or exchanges seamlessly.
  • Maintain our 100% customer retention rate by providing personalized future recommendations.

Financial Security

We never store your direct financial details on our servers. All transactions—including our 50/50 split payments—are processed entirely through industry-leading, encrypted gateways such as PayPal, Zelle, ApplePay, and direct Credit Card processors. Your financial security is guaranteed by these world-class institutions.

No Data Selling

Your personal data is an exclusive asset of your relationship with Omega Hub Online. We explicitly do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third parties, marketing agencies, or data brokers under any circumstances.

Third-Party Sharing

We only share the absolute minimum necessary information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in fulfilling your order. Specifically, we provide your name, shipping address, and contact number to FedEx to ensure the swift, global delivery of your package from our vaults in Dubai and Hong Kong. Customs agencies may also require basic details to process international imports legally.

Your Rights & Contacting Us

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. If you wish to manage your data, update your shipping preferences, or have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our dedicated team via Instagram DM or WhatsApp. We are always here to assist you.
