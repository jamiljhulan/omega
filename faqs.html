Shipping & Returns

Do you ship worldwide and how long does it take?

Yes! While 95% of our clients are based in the USA, followed by Canada and Australia, we proudly offer global delivery. Once you approve your pre-shipment video, your package is dispatched via FedEx Expedited Shipping and typically arrives at your door within 7 Working Days.

Will I have to pay customs duties or import taxes?

Because your items are shipped directly from our international vaults, your package may occasionally be subject to local customs fees. We optimize our shipping to minimize border friction, but any localized import duties mandated by your country are the responsibility of the buyer.

Your peace of mind is guaranteed. While our Video Approval process ensures clients are always thrilled with their items, we do offer a fully guaranteed return and refund policy. If you are dissatisfied upon receiving your piece, simply DM us to initiate an easy, hassle-free return and receive a full refund to your original payment method.
