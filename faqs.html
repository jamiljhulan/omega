Concierge & Protocol

Everything you need to know about navigating the Omega Hub Private Archive, from offer negotiation to VIP delivery.

Are the designer bags and luxury items authentic? +

Yes. Every piece in the Omega Hub Private Archive undergoes rigorous, multi-point authentication by luxury experts at our Dubai hub before being listed. We guarantee 100% authenticity for all brands, including Hermès, Chanel, and Louis Vuitton.

How do I purchase an item from the Vault? +

We operate exclusively through a VIP Instagram Concierge model to ensure a personalized luxury experience. Simply click "DM TO NEGOTIATE" or "DM TO BUY" on any product, and our styling team will finalize your order securely via direct message.

Can I negotiate the price of a designer item? +

Yes. We welcome private offers. Because we operate as a direct-to-buyer archive without traditional retail markups, we respect your budget and encourage reasonable negotiations on our vault pieces.

How long does VIP shipping take to California and Florida? +

We offer complimentary expedited shipping to the US. Once your item passes final quality inspection and packaging at our fulfillment center, it typically arrives to clients in California or Florida within 4 to 7 business days.

What is your return policy on archive pieces? +

Due to the exclusive, highly sought-after nature of our sourced inventory, all private archive sales are final. However, we provide detailed high-resolution videos and condition reports via our Instagram DM concierge prior to purchase to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Our VIP Concierge team is available 24/7 to assist you with sourcing, authentication details, or shipping inquiries.
