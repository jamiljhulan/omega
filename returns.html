To ensure you absolutely love your piece, we proactively prevent issues before they happen. Before any order leaves our warehouses in Dubai or Hong Kong, we send you a detailed, high-definition video of your specific item. We only ship once you give us your final approval.

Secure Processing

All financial transactions—whether it is your initial purchase, a 50/50 split, or a return—are handled with the utmost security. We process all refunds directly back to your original payment method, including PayPal, Zelle, ApplePay, or Credit Card. With our guaranteed refund policy, you can shop our international vault with absolute confidence.

Return Conditions

To qualify for a full refund or exchange, items must be returned in the exact premium condition they were received. This includes all original tags, protective covers, dust bags, and boxes. Items that show signs of wear, damage, or alteration will not be eligible for return.
